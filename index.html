Our Values


MISSION: The school was established on 9th march of 1997 to provide a quality education and all-round development of the rural pupils in this periphery. The concious and honest effort of it's director nurtured so many alumni to prestigeous destinations. The main focus of the establisher of this school is not only to build careers of his desciples, but to boost them up to their moral and ethical heights and moulding them into complete and practical personalities.
